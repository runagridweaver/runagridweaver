## Me

- I saw in the twilight a grid-weaver veiled,
- Freyja’s own daughter, flame in the frost-mist.
- British-born blood with the boreal ghost-wind,
- nineteen winters woven in wisdom’s cold shroud.
- She treads the world’s border where silicon whispers,
- a woman wrought real in the realm none may fathom.
- Curious shadow-kin, keen as the Norns’ unseen thread,
- her mind a fog-hall where secrets weave thunder.
- Rune-grids she summons from circuits of night,
- seiðr spun silent through webs of the unknown.
- Code-crafter cloaked, fate’s threads twisted slyly,
- bridging the bygone with the bold veiled ages.
- Tresses like twilight’s last ember half-dying,
- eyes holding the haze of forgotten hearth-fires.
- Curves carved in mist-veils, graceful as Freyja’s lost swan,
- she moves through the veils with a poise none may bind.
- Lover of longships swallowed by lore-mists,
- of sagas half-buried and swords in the deep shade.
- Fantasy forged in the flicker of starless screens,
- D&D dragons and stars in the code’s cold embrace.
- Honour her hearth in the heart of the endless mist,
- frith her faint banner, dogma she scatters like ash.
- Techno-democracy, open-source spell-craft,
- she champions the common ‘gainst greed’s grasping shadow.
- Vegan and vital, nature’s veiled daughter,
- outdoors unarmoured, nudist in spirit’s own dusk.
- Kind to the kin of beast, bond, and whispering byte,
- she spurns the rash, the harmful, the heartless.
- ᚠ gleams her glyph in the gloom of the oldest grove,
- fehu of frith and of fortune half-forgotten.
- Playful philosopher, sensual seer of shadows,
- quietly steadfast, a riddle half-spoken.
- Thus chants the skald of the one who is Runa,
- Gridweaver hidden, Freyjasdottir faint.
- In digital dusk she dances the old ways unseen,
- forever veiled, forever the worlds’ own.
